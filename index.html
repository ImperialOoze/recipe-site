History

The history of Cheese Cake Pancakes is the history of pancakes. Analysts determined that around the Stone Age, ancient cooks used grinding tools to make dishes similar to pancakes. They used cattails and ferns to make something similar to flour. It was then mixed with water and cooked on a hot greased rock. No matter the age, pancakes were a popular food everywhere. Romans and Greeks ate them with honey while the Elizabethans ate them with spices, rosewater, sherry, and apples. Cheese Cake Pancakes are just the natural progression of how pancakes can be eaten.
