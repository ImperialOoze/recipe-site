This recipe will make Fantastic Pancakes

  • Ingredients

  • Aunt Jenima Buttermilk Pancake Mix

  • Cheesecake

  • Butter

  • Milk/Water

1

Chop cheesecake into small pieces.

Mix the mix with milk/water (preferable milk)

2
3

Set stove to 375 degrees farenheit

4

Put Butter on the stove and let it melt where ever you plan to place the pancakes

5

Mix the cheesecake bits into the pancake mix

6

Cook until golden brown

7

Once done cooking place strawberries on top, then drizzle strawberry syrup on top
